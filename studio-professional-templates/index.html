Studio Professional Templates

iOS, Android, TV

The Studio Professional Templates are a comprehensive package of UI templates. These templates help both the Sales team and Product Success and Integration team at Accedo sell and deliver Studio Professional client apps.

These templates were created to help minimize the amount of time required in both the design and app configuration phase. Clients could look through detailed design documentation, and select the screen layouts that they wanted to use for each section of their video application. A client provides their branding guidelines and a site map, and then Accedo designers could then easily take the Sketch templates, with all the screens for each platform, and easily update the Sketch file with the client’s branding elements using Symbols, Text Styles, and Layer Styles.
