Party Crasher

Game

Party Crasher is a simple Flash game with a 8-bit art style. The concept is simple. Users play as the “Party Crasher” character and show up uninvited to each level’s party.

The goal of each level is to steal the cake without any party-goers spotting you to advance to the next level. Users can collect other items such as balloons, gifts, and cocktails for bonus points along the way as well.

Development by Alex Hughes.
