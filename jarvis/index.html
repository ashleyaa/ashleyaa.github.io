Jarvis

Alexa, TV

Jarvis was created to help address a common office issue; a lack of available meeting rooms. Using a TV app in the common area, colleagues can view meeting room availabilty, and book rooms using a series of voice commands with Amazon’s Alexa. 

Users are able to view meeting room availability, along with who has booked the rooms. Users can also use Jarvis to message to users on Slack with voice to see if they still need the room as well.

Jarvis was created for Digiflare voice Hackathon. Development by Alex Hughes, Ricardo Sabino, and Filip Szulczewski.
